CalorieTracker

Your personal AI-powered nutrition assistant.

CalorieTracker is a modern, intuitive web application designed to help you take control of your nutritional health. By leveraging the power of AI, we provide personalized diet plans, in-depth analysis of your eating habits, and motivational support to keep you on track with your health goals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make nutrition tracking simple, accessible, and effective for everyone. We believe that with the right tools and insights, anyone can achieve a healthier lifestyle. Whether you're looking to lose weight, build muscle, or simply eat better, CalorieTracker is here to guide you every step of the way.
